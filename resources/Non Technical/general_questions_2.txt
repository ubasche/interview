
General Interview Questions (Interviewer)
===========================

- Tell us about yourself/ your background. 
- What motivates you to do a good job? 
- What qualities do you think are essential to be successful in 
  this kind of work? 
- What courses did you attend? What job certifications do you hold? 
- What subjects/courses did you excel in? Why? 
- What subjects/courses gave you trouble? Why? 
- How does your previous work experience prepare you for this position? 
- How do you define 'success'? 
- What has been your most significant accomplishment to date? 
- Share an incident when you failed in an endeavor. 
- Tell us about a problem you encountered in your work.  How did 
  you handle it? 
- Describe the 'ideal' job... the 'ideal' supervisor. 
- What leadership roles have you held? 
- What prejudices do you hold? 
- What do you like to do in your spare time? 
- What are your long-range career goals? 
- How does this position match your career goals? 
- In what areas do you feel you need further education and 
  training to be successful? 
- What do you know about our company? 
- Why do you want to work for this company. Why should we hire you? 
- Where do you see yourself fitting in to this organization 
  . . .initially? . . .in 5 years? 
- Why are you looking for a new job? 
- How do you feel about re-locating? 
- Are you willing to travel? 
- What are your salary requirements? 
- When would you be available to start if you were selected? 


Work with the CV

- Mark all interesting things on the CV before the interview.
- Write the CV specific questions on the CV next to the related 
  sentence in the CV.
- Calculate the length of the previous jobs and write it on the
  CV.


Questions towards the CV

- How did the upgrad from Oracle 7 to 8i go? Any problems?
- What are your experiences with RMAN?
- What kind of company is 'name of company'?
- You stayed only 3 month at 'company'. Why did you leave?



General Interview Questions (Candidate)
===========================

- Do you have a training program for each employee?
- What projects are comming up?
- What technologies are used by the company?
- How big is the team / development department?
- How are the working time arangements?
- What will my place / responcibilities in the team be?
- What is you personal point of view why I should join the company?
